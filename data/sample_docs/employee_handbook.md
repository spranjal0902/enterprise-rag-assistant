# Acme Corp Employee Handbook (Excerpt)

## Working Hours and Flexibility
Standard working hours are 09:00 to 17:30, Monday to Friday. Employees may adopt
flexible start times between 07:30 and 10:00 with manager approval, provided core
hours (10:00 to 16:00) are covered. Remote work is permitted up to three days per
week for roles that do not require on-site presence.

## Code of Conduct
All employees are expected to act with integrity and treat colleagues, customers,
and partners with respect. Harassment, discrimination, and retaliation are strictly
prohibited. Violations should be reported to People Operations or through the
confidential ethics line.

## Expense Reimbursement
Business expenses must be submitted within 30 days of being incurred. Receipts are
required for any expense above 25 EUR. Travel bookings should be made through the
approved corporate travel portal. Reimbursements are processed in the next payroll
cycle after approval.

## Equipment
Each employee is issued a company laptop and may request a monitor, keyboard, and
headset. Hardware faults should be reported to the IT Service Desk. Lost or stolen
equipment must be reported within 24 hours.
