# Acme Analytics Platform - Internal FAQ (Excerpt)

## What is the Acme Analytics Platform?
The Acme Analytics Platform is an internal tool for building dashboards and
reports from company data sources. It connects to the data warehouse and supports
scheduled refreshes, role-based access, and exportable reports.

## How do I get access?
Request access through the IT Service Desk and specify the data domains you need
(for example, Sales, Finance, or Operations). Access is granted based on your role
and is reviewed quarterly. Restricted data domains require additional approval from
the data owner.

## How often is data refreshed?
Most dashboards refresh every 24 hours overnight. Near-real-time dashboards for
operations refresh every 15 minutes. If a dashboard appears stale, check the
refresh status indicator before contacting support.

## Who do I contact for help?
For access and permissions, contact the IT Service Desk. For questions about
metric definitions or report logic, contact the Data Analytics team through the
internal support channel. Bugs should be filed in the issue tracker with a
screenshot and the dashboard URL.
