# Acme Corp Information Security Policy (Excerpt)

## Passwords and Authentication
All accounts must use a password of at least 12 characters combining letters,
numbers, and symbols. Multi-factor authentication (MFA) is mandatory for email,
VPN, and any system that handles customer data. Passwords must be changed if a
compromise is suspected and must never be shared.

## Data Classification
Company data is classified as Public, Internal, Confidential, or Restricted.
Confidential and Restricted data must be encrypted at rest and in transit.
Restricted data, such as customer financial records, may only be accessed by
authorized personnel on a need-to-know basis.

## Device Security
Company laptops must have full-disk encryption and the approved endpoint
protection agent enabled. Operating systems and software must be kept up to date.
Personal devices may access company email only if enrolled in mobile device
management.

## Incident Reporting
Any suspected security incident, including phishing emails, malware, or lost
devices, must be reported to the Security Operations Center within one hour of
discovery. Do not attempt to investigate suspected malware on your own.
