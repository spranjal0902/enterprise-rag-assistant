# Acme Corp Paid Time Off (PTO) Policy (Excerpt)

## Annual Leave
Full-time employees accrue 28 days of paid annual leave per calendar year,
accrued monthly. Up to 5 unused days may be carried over into the following year
and must be used by 31 March, after which they expire.

## Requesting Leave
Leave requests should be submitted through the HR portal at least two weeks in
advance for planned absences. Manager approval is required. During peak business
periods, the company may limit the number of team members on leave at the same
time.

## Sick Leave
Employees should notify their manager as early as possible on the first day of
illness. A medical certificate is required for absences longer than three
consecutive working days. Sick leave does not count against annual leave.

## Parental Leave
Eligible employees are entitled to parental leave in line with statutory
requirements. Requests should be discussed with People Operations at least eight
weeks before the intended start date to arrange handover and cover.
